Chicken Katsu Curry

Description

Ez Pz fried chicken cutlets served over a warm curry.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • egg
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • water
  • box of curry
  • carrots
  • onions
  • potatoes

Instructions

  1. Pound and season your chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Dip into flour, then egg, and finally panko breadcrumbs.
  2. Cut up your vegtables and sear them in a pot with oil.
  3. Prepare the curry according to the instructions on the back.
  4. While the curry is cooking fry up your chicken.
  5. Serve over rice.
